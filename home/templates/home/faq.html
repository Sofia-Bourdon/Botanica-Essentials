Frequently Asked Questions:

Are your products suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin?

Absolutely! Our formulations are designed to cater to all skin types. However, we always recommend patch-testing any new product to ensure it's a perfect match for your skin.

What natural ingredients are primarily used in your skincare products?

Our products are crafted with a blend of organic aloe vera, jojoba oil, green tea extracts, and soothing essential oils like lavender and chamomile to ensure nature's best for your skin.

I have dry skin, especially in the winter. Which of your products would you recommend for deep hydration?

For dry skin, especially in winter, our Aloe line is a good choice. It's formulated for sensitive, dry skin, offering that deep hydration needed.

Do you test on animals?

No, at Botanica Essentials, we're committed to cruelty-free practices. Our products are never tested on animals.

Are there any preservatives in your products?

While we focus on natural ingredients, we use minimal preservatives to ensure the longevity and safety of our products, always ensuring they are safe and skin-friendly.

Can I return a product I'm not happy with?

Absolutely! Return unopened items within 30 days for a full refund. Just ensure they're in original packaging. Need help? Contact our team!
